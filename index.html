9Foundations

What is 9Foundations?

9Foundations, based off of 8values is a moral and political quiz that attempts to assign percentages on nine different moral values. You will be presented by a statement, and then you will answer with your opinion on the statement, from Strongly Agree to Strongly Disagree, with each answer slightly affecting your scores. At the end of the quiz, your answers will be compared to the maximum possible for each value, thus giving you a percentage. Answer honestly!

What are the nine moral values?


Care / Harm
Those who score higher in Care prioritize cherishing and protecting others. It embraces virtues like kindness, gentleness, and nurturance. They tend to be more Leftist.


Fairness / Inequity
Those who score higher in Fairness prioritize equal treatment under the law, making society more equal, and a equitable distribution of wealth and social justice. It embraces virtues like justice, cooperation, and fairness. They tend to be more Leftist.


Liberty / Oppression
Those who score higher in Liberty prioritize freedom, and resent authorities that restrict liberty. It embraces virtues like freedom, and self-determination. They tend to be more Libertarian.


In-group loyalty / In-group betrayal
Those who score higher in In-group royalty prioritize standing with your group, family, nation. It embraces virtues like patriotism and community. They tend to be more Patriotic or Tribalist.


Honor / Disgrace
Those who score higher in Honor prioritize honor, respect and self-discipline. It embraces virtues like self-discipline, dignity, and self-sacrifice. They tend to be more royal.


Divinity / Impiety
Those who score higher in Divinity prioritize spirituality and religion. It embraces virtues like spirituality, purposefulness, and dignity. They tend to be more Religious.


Chastity / Degradation
Those who score higher in Chastity prioritize standards of "high society" and avoid behavior that is percieved as primitive, degenerate, recklessly hedonistic. It embraces virtues like purity, modesty, and cleanliness. They tend to be more Traditional.


Authority / Subversion
Those who score higher in Authority prioritize submitting to laws and legitimate authority. It embraces virtues like lawfulness, followeship, and discipline. They tend to be more Authoritarian.


Efficiency / Waste
Those who score higher in Efficiency prioritize not wasting food and resource. It embraces virtues like frugality. They tend to be more anti-consumerist.
